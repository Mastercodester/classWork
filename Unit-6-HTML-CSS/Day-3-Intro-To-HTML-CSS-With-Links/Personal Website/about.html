About Me

My name is Aaron Jackson, and I’m an entrepreneur, consultant, and creative based in Detroit, Michigan.

With a passion for business, technology, and music, I’ve built my career around helping others turn their ideas into successful ventures.

I hold a degree in Business Management from Eastern Michigan University, and I’ve combined my education with real-world experience to guide clients through business development, tech projects, and creative industries.

Through my consulting services, I provide hands-on support in business strategy, tech development, and the music industry.

Whether it's helping a startup launch, developing an app, building a brand, or assisting artists with engineering, video production, distribution, and marketing, I bring a full-service approach to every project.

Growing up in Detroit has shaped my mission to give back to my community by creating job opportunities, mentoring young entrepreneurs, and investing in local talent.

My ultimate goal is to use my skills and experience to help others succeed while building businesses that create positive impact and long-term growth.
