Welcome to my portfolio — a showcase of the projects and clients I’ve worked with across business consulting, technology, and music.

Each project reflects my hands-on approach, creative problem-solving, and commitment to helping individuals and businesses succeed.

Muddies

I launched and operated Muddies Soulfood, a food truck business that allowed me to apply my business management skills in real-time.

I handled everything from business planning, menu creation, vendor relationships, and daily operations to customer service, marketing, and financial management.

Through this venture, I gained valuable experience in small business ownership, team leadership, and adapting to the fast-paced food industry, all of which I now use to help other entrepreneurs grow and succeed.

Tech Projects

As part of my tech consulting work, I’ve led and contributed to several software development projects.

A key project was my Farm to Table App (FACTS App), which connects local farmers to consumers, promoting access to fresh produce and supporting local economies.

I also developed a Library Management System during my training, which allowed users to check book availability and track inventory.

Through these projects, I gained deep experience in app design, development processes, user interface planning, and project management, giving me the tools to help clients build, refine, and launch their own tech products.

Music

Under my artist name Smokkey P, I’ve created, engineered, and released original music, gaining hands-on experience with the full music production and release cycle.

I manage my own recording sessions, mixing, mastering, and collaborate with video teams to shoot professional visuals for my songs.

I’ve successfully distributed my music across major streaming platforms while building marketing strategies to grow my audience.

This real-world experience allows me to guide other artists through recording, production, visual content creation, digital distribution, and brand development.
